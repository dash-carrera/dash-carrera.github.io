Participation

ENGL1105 is a course, but it is also a community. For any community to thrive, we need to be able to depend on one another. That means that for discussions, peer reviews, workshop, and other forms of work in which you will be helping and learning from one another, I expect you to be present, dependable, and cordial. Please be a good and respectful class citizen. Anything less will adversely effect your participation grade.

Journal

You will each be asked to maintain a journal during this course in the form of a Google Doc, shared with me. Journal entries will be made every day at the beginning of class in response to prompts. Your journal will be graded for its display of effort and whether all entries have been completed. The journal is a place for you to try new things with your writing practice, and to receive feedback on your writing outside of the traditional grading cycle.
