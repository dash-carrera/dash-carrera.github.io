Calendar

Class

Class will take place from 8:00-8:50am on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday in McBryde 328.

Mondays - Lab/Activity

We will work on developing specific skills which will be useful for your upcoming projects. Occasionally, I will give a brief lecture.

Wednesdays - Discussion

These dicussions will be about an assigned reading. Your reading respone is due at 8:00AM each Tuesday.These responses are to be pasted into a group Google Doc, the link for which you can find below. Two students will be named discussion leaders each week, and will be responsible for reading everyone's responses, bringing in at least 5 questions for discussion, and keeping the discussion going.

Fridays - Project Day

On project days we will do an activity which will become part of your project. These activities may take the form of brainstorms, peer review, or something else.

Journal

We will be doing prompted journal writing for 10 minutes at the beginning of each class. This writing must go into a Google Doc in your Google Drive folder.
