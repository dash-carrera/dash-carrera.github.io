Calendar

Class

Class will take place from 8:00-8:50am on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday in McBryde 328.

Mondays - Lab/Activity

We will work on developing specific skills which will be useful for your upcoming projects. Occasionally, I will give a brief lecture.

Wednesdays - Discussion

These dicussions will be about an assigned reading. Your reading respone is due at 8:00AM each Tuesday and must be at least 250 words. These responses are to be pasted into a group Google Doc, the link for which you can find here. If you miss the Tuesday 8:00AM deadline, you can still receive half credit by emailing me a reading reponse by Friday at 8:00AM for half credit. Two students will be named discussion leaders each week, and will be responsible for reading everyone's responses, keeping the discussion going, and trying to address the questions I have raised.

Fridays - Project Day

On project days we will do an activity which will become part of your project. These activities may take the form of brainstorms, peer review, or something else.

Journal

We will be doing prompted journal writing for 10 minutes at the beginning of each class. This writing must go into a Google Doc in your Google Drive folder. Each week, I will provide light, ungraded feedback on your journal.

IMPORTANT

PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS SCHEDULE IS TENTATIVE AND SUBJECT TO CHANGE.
