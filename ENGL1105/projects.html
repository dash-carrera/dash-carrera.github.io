Project 3: Mixed Media Rhetorical Analysis

Project 3 is a mixed-media rhetorical analysis of an academic paper. You will constuct a small website to present this work using Google Sites. The text component of your analysis must be at least 1,750 words (~7 pages). There must be 4 pieces of original media present on your site. You will turn in 3 drafts of this project. The first must have at least one completed body section, including media. The second must include at least two completed body sections. The third must include at least three completed body sections.
